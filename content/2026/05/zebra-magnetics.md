Title: Zebra Magnetics
Date: 2026-05-09
Tags: gear, edc

As [mentioned previously](/2025/02/window-film-flood/), two years ago I bought a [ZebraLight H600c Mk IV](https://www.zebralight.com/H600c-Mk-IV-18650-XHP502-4000K-High-CRI-Headlamp_p_235.html) headlamp. When I was ordering the light, [The Internet also suggested that I buy magnets for it](https://old.reddit.com/r/flashlight/comments/hktb36/psa_adding_a_tailcap_magnet_to_a_zebralight/). Reader, I did.

<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pigmonkey/55260086879/in/dateposted/" title="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Tail Cap Magnet"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/55260086879_bc75ab186d_c.jpg" width="800" height="533" alt="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Tail Cap Magnet"/></a>

From K&J Magnetics I bought the [D82-N52](https://www.kjmagnetics.com/d82-n52-neodymium-disc-magnet). This is a 1/2" x 1/8" N52 neodymium disc magnet. It can be slid inside the spring of the tail cap. It does not interfere with the function of the cap. The flat top 18650 still fits, and the cap still screws on all the way.

<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pigmonkey/55258955562/in/dateposted/" title="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Tail Cap Magnet"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/55258955562_a0ba4e482e_c.jpg" width="800" height="533" alt="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Tail Cap Magnet"/></a>

I also bought the [BX842](https://www.kjmagnetics.com/bx842-neodymium-block-magnet). This is a 1 1/2" x 1/4" x 1/8" N42 neodymium block magnet. It can be attached to the pocket clip, and then secured with heat-shrink tubing.

<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pigmonkey/55259991258/in/dateposted/" title="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Pocket Clip Magnet"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/55259991258_a238ca333e_c.jpg" width="800" height="533" alt="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Pocket Clip Magnet"/></a>

I installed these magnets as soon as I got the light. I haven't regretted it. The only minor annoyance is that when I ride to the beach and set my helmet down in the sand, the pocket clip magnet collects all the iron in the sand. I don't like sand. It's coarse and rough and irritating and it gets everywhere.

<a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/pigmonkey/55260256785/in/dateposted/" title="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Helmet Mount"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/55260256785_bf552205ca_c.jpg" width="800" height="533" alt="ZebraLight H600c Mk IV: Helmet Mount"/></a>

While the H600c is primarily a headlamp, I do occasionally carry it in my pocket, and I've yet to be annoyed by the magnets. If it was a dedicated pocket light, perhaps I'd feel differently.
