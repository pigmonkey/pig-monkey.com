About

I'm just some guy, you know?

I value autonomy, movement, and diversity. I have a tendency to be distrustful of authority. I prefer decentralized, rhizomatic networks to centralized systems. I dislike writing autobiography.

The best way to learn about me is to explore my writings. This website functions as the central portal for my online presence. It is a record of many goings and comings, doings and beings; stories, symbols and meanings. Gossamer threads that may be woven into a larger web: a story of this age of the world.

The website is powered by Python, hand-forged in Vim. It utilizes Django, Vellum, Sass, Compass, and Susy. The code is entirely open-source and available on GitHub.
